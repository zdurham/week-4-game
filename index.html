Rupee Collector

Back from a long adventure, you are now looking to buy some unopened treasure chests from a local village. However, they only take exact change!

Unfortunately, these shopkeepers also value rupees differently, so you don't know how much they will be valued until you hand them over.

The desired amount will be displayed below. Press one of the rupees to build up your score. The goal is to reach the desired amount exactly. If you go over you will have an ornery shopkeeper on your hands!

Be careful! After each successful (or failed) transaction, the values of the rupees will randomly change!
